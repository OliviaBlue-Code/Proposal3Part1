Our tufting business aims to provide high quality, custom tufted products to customers. The website will showcase our products, share our story, and provide a seamless online shopping experience.


Mission


Our mission is to empower creativity and bring warmth to homes through handcrafted, bespoke tufted pieces that exceed our customers' expectations. We're dedicated to preserving traditional craftsmanship while pushing the boundaries of modern tufting design, ensuring each piece is a work of art. At our business, our mission is to build a community that values handmade, sustainable, and unique home decor, one tuft at a time.

Vision


Our vision is to become the go-to destination for bespoke tufted furniture, renowned for our attention to detail, innovative designs, and commitment to sustainability. We envision a world where handmade, artisanal pieces are cherished for their beauty, quality, and story, and where our customers' homes reflect their personal style. We're committed to growing our business while staying true to our roots, always prioritizing quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.

Organization History


Founded in 2021, TuftAway was born out of a passion for traditional craftsmanship and a desire to create unique, handmade pieces that bring warmth and character to homes. Our journey began with a simple love for tufting from tiktok videos and a dream to own equipment, create and share that with others. Today, we're proud to have grown into a team of skilled artisans dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and service. From humble beginnings to a thriving business, TuftAway has always been driven by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
